Did you know that Lingnan has a dental plan? Yes, it exists.

I have personally used the service for scaling (teeth cleaning) and filling. The cost was approximately: $100 for scaling + $600 for one white filling = $700+.

The coverage period is from September 1, 2025, to August 31, 2026 (subject to update for the 26-27 academic year).

### How to book an appointment
1. For scaling, you must call the dedicated hotline: 2120 6134. Remember to state whether you are a full-time student and provide your SID.
2. For other services, you need to contact the specific branch directly. Details can be found here: https://health-care.com.hk/

My experience was that the staff are friendly, and there is no hard selling.

**Important Reminders:**

1. It is best to book one or two weeks in advance.
2. You must cancel your appointment at least 24 hours in advance.
3. If you 'no-show' twice, your membership will be suspended for three months.
4. Do not visit if you have a cold.
