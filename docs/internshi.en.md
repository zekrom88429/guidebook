# Internship Opportunities at Lingnan University

Since the market is bringing more and more concerns in practical experience,
internship is more important. Therefore, today I would like to introduce the internships available at Lingnan University.

No matter what year you are in. I think you can still apply for internship opportunities.

## ITC stem internship programe

link: https://www.ln.edu.hk/osa/career-planning/internship-placements/local-internships/stem-internship-scheme

Mode: Full time 
Place: Local On-site internship
Days : 28-59 consecutive days

Caution: Non-local students quota are capped at 40% 

This programe aims to help students to be equipped with innovation and technology (“I&T”). Like coding, data analytics, 3D modelling.

The registration of the program starts before April 27. You might need to sacrifice some practice time for the exam to tailor your resume.

On 27 April, there will be a Zoom session that talks about the program information and some necessary information on jobs.

After the Zoom session, an application form link will be given, and then you will need to fill out the application form before or on June 14. The faster you submit, the higher the chances of being nominated for an interview.

Inside the application, you are required to provide some basic personal information, a resume, specify your takeaways from the internship, and choose 3 job roles from the job list.


Pro tip: You should only upload a general resume for the highest ROI, but if you are really hungry for that interview, then you should email:
sinmanlau@ln.edu.hk (Miss Lau Sin Man, Annie) or cynthialam@ln.edu.hk (Miss Lam Po Ying, Cynthia) for uploading more CVs that tailor to each job description.

If you are shortlisted for an internship opportunity, then before the internship starts, you must do 3 things of internship preparation listed on the website. Which mitigates you from internship traps.
