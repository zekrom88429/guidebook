1. Get involved in various activities: There are countless events on campus. They are great opportunities to meet new people and build your network. Aim for leadership roles, such as becoming a club president, or participate in competitions.
2. Build strong relationships with professors: Make a conscious effort to connect with your instructors, especially those within your own faculty.
3. Keep your GPA high: A strong GPA is essential for securing scholarships and awards. Aim for at least a 3.5.
4. Initiate your own projects: Take the initiative to start your own projects or ventures to showcase your drive and creativity.
5. Expand your social circle: Don’t just stick to your own faculty. Make an effort to connect with students from diverse backgrounds; it will broaden your perspective and enrich your understanding of the world.
