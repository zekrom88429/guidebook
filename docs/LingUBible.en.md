 **[LingUBible](https://www.lingubible.com/)** is a platform built by a Lingnan student, for Lingnan students, already trusted by **200+ students**. It helps you **choose courses smarter**, **plan your semester faster**, and **stress less about grades**.

![meta-image](img/lingubible/meta-image.png)

## 🌟 LingUBible Key Features 🌟

### **Read & write course/instructor reviews**
Browse honest reviews from students who've actually taken the course, before you register. Took a course yourself? Share your experience to help the next batch of students avoid picking blind.
![](img/lingubible/read-review.png)

![[img/lingubible/write-review.png]]

### **View & download syllabi and past exam papers**
No more hunting around — course syllabi and past exam papers are all searchable in one place.
![[img/lingubible/syllabi-pp.png]]

### **Timetable Planner**
Select sessions and preview your weekly schedule before you commit, so you can spot clashes and gaps before add/drop period even starts.
![[img/lingubible/timetable.png]]

### **GPA Calculator & Honours Stats**
Instantly calculate your GPA and see where it places you in the honours classification — know where you stand, anytime.
![[img/lingubible/gpa.png]]

![[img/lingubible/hons.png]]

### **Lingnan Academic Calendar**
Check key term dates and deadlines at a glance, without digging through the university website.
![[img/lingubible/calendar.png]]
